About Me

Hi! I'm Roger Torres, an 18-year-old freshman studying Computer Science at the University of Pittsburgh. I built this website as a way to grow my skills as a developer — and I plan for it to evolve as I do.

I enjoy building things that actually do something, whether that's solving a problem, exploring an idea, or just learning a new concept along the way. I'm especially interested in full-stack development and how software, logic, and design come together to create real products.

Outside of coding, I'm a big fan of movies and TV — Mr. Robot, The Wire, The Sopranos, BoJack Horseman, and Gravity Falls are some favorites. I also enjoy anime and manga, especially Berserk, Vinland Saga, Dragon Ball Z, Tokyo Ghoul, Bleach, and Naruto.

When I'm not watching something, I'm probably gaming (too much Valorant, and forever loyal to ARK: Survival Evolved), playing pickup basketball, or working out — staying active is something I really value. I also enjoy dipping into philosophy from time to time.

Looking ahead, I'm excited to keep building new programming projects, learning more about software engineering, and taking the next steps toward independence.
